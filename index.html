PAUL GRAHAM

English programmer /Venture Capitalist/ Essayist

Hey there, nice to meet you !

Paul Graham is known for his work on Lisp, for co-founding Viaweb (which eventually became Yahoo Store), and for co-founding the Y Combinator seed capital firm. He is the author of some programming books, such as: On Lisp(1993), ANSI Common Lisp(1995), and Hackers & Painters(2004).
